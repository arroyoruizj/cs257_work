The Three Doors of Destiny

While walking in the arb, you come across three mysterious doors, each adorned with intricate carvings that seem to whisper secrets of the unknown. A sense of anticipation fills the air as you realize the behind one of the doors lies one million dollars, while behind the other two lurk a pit of venomous snakes, eager to ensnare unwary passersby.

Are you willing to take the risk?
